Ingredients:
Spaghetti: 200g
Ground beef: 500g
Onion: 1
Garlic: 2 cloves
Tomato sauce: 400g
Olive oil: 2 tbsp
Salt: to taste
Pepper: to taste
Basil: 1 tsp

Preparation:
1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil and cook the ground beef until browned.
3. Add the onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
4. Stir in the tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and basil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.
